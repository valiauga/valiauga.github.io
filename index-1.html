[vas - kii - chii]
Lithuanian take on an otherwise timeless ‘paper-scissors-rock’ game. Instead of a rock, its a well…

No one knows why it’s a well, but it goes like this; ‘Scissors fall into a well - paper covers the well, paper also doesn’t stand a chance agaist the scissors.

Traditionally used in solving disputes, trading, perhaps even making marriage proposals; This time, it is a way of testing one’s fortune playing against nothing else but a heartless machine…

Hover over the colourful type to select a sign, then click the type to cast your chance! Press a ‘lighting’ button to reset the score, then play again...
