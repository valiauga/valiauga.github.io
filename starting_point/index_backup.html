Starting Point

Immersive soundscape exhibition project created for MO, Modern art museum in Vilnius, Lithuania. The project takes visitors on a journey through artist’s creative process, enabling the public to discover unique starting points that, in one way or another, led the artists shown to creating their artwork.

Interactive objects represent each artwork. Triggered, these objects come to life, each telling visitor a story about an art piece and an artist’s journey to it.

About project

“Finding new ways to engage the public is a challenge that even the biggest museums and cultural institutions are facing today. Yet, for MO Museum – a new modern art museum in Vilnius, Lithuania – this challenge is their mission.”
